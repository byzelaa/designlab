Time destroys…
Portrait photography

Time is one of the most powerful invisible elements in which people are affected by, this is why people should be aware of the power of time more. Some consequences of time are obviously inevitable as people are getting older and heading towards the end of their lives minute by minute. As time is invisible, these events are carried out unconsciously and too subtle to be aware of them. People cannot predict what will happen to them therefore we can’t change what we don’t know. Nevertheless, there are some consequences of time people can’t prevent. Throughout this project, I intend to remind people why time is essential by demonstrating consequences of time such as death, hardship, and loss of memory.
